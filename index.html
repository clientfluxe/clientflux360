Have questions before getting started?

Here are the answers agents usually ask before building their lead system.

What mainly do you set up for real estate agent?

We set up lead capture, CRM pipelines, automated follow-up, appointment booking, and tracking systems for buyer and seller inquiries.

Do I need to already have a CRM?

No. We can work with your current CRM or help structure a clean setup from scratch.

Will this help me get more real estate leads?

It helps you capture, organize, and convert leads better. For more leads, you can connect this system with ads, social pages, and listing traffic.

Can this work for both buyers and sellers?

Yes. We can create different pipelines, follow-ups, and booking steps for buyer leads and seller leads.

What happens when a new lead comes in?

The system can notify you, organize the lead, trigger follow-up, and guide the prospect toward a call or showing.

How long does setup take?

Timeline depends on your package, CRM, and workflow. Simple setups can be completed faster, while advanced systems need more configuration.
